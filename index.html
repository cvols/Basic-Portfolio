About Me

Hi! My name is Christopher Volonnino; born and raised in New Jersey. Grew up an ice hockey fan, playing all across North America, including Wisconsin, Nebraska, and British Columbia, Canada. I eventually went to the University of Delaware where I graduated with a Sport Management degree. I have been working for about two and a half years as a Business Development and Sales Account Manager for Apex Systems. From there, I realized how much I enjoyed coding and technology. For the past year plus, I've been coding and developing my own websites on the side. I've decided its time to take a bootcamp and get a certification so I can get a job and get paid to code! Looking for a position that will allow me to be more creative and solve problems.


I still haven't decided exactly what I want to do with technology. I'm torn between web development and mobile development. I would like to start an iOS class as well to see how much I like that. I'm pretty open for my first job, but would really like to work for a start up company, ideally in Hoboken if possible.


Can't wait to see where this takes me!
